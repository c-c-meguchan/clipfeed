# ClipFeed

**Never lose what you copied.**

ClipFeed is a minimal clipboard timeline for macOS that keeps the things you copy so you can quickly recover them when you need them.

It runs quietly in your menu bar and automatically saves your clipboard history — helping you grab something you copied a few seconds or minutes ago.

![ClipFeed Screenshot](./screenshots/main.png)

## Download

Download the latest version from **Releases**.

→ https://github.com/c-c-meguchan/clipfeed/releases

Just open the `.dmg` and move ClipFeed to your Applications folder.

## Why ClipFeed?

We all lose things we just copied.

You copy something  
Then copy something else  
And suddenly the previous one is gone.

ClipFeed acts as a **clipboard safety net**, letting you quickly recover anything you copied before.

No setup, no complex features — just your clipboard history when you need it.

## Features

• Clipboard timeline of recently copied items  
• Menu bar app with quick access  
• OCR from screenshots  
• Filter history by source application  
• Light / Dark theme support  

ClipFeed is designed to stay lightweight and out of your way.

## Example Use Cases

• Recover text you accidentally overwrote  
• Grab something you copied a minute ago  
• Copy text from screenshots using OCR  
• Keep temporary text while working  
• Quickly reuse links or messages

ClipFeed is especially useful during fast workflows like coding, writing, or research.

## Privacy by Design

ClipFeed is built with an **offline-first design**.

Your clipboard data **never leaves your Mac**.

No clipboard data is sent to any server.  
Network access is only used to check for application updates.

## Philosophy

ClipFeed is intentionally minimal.

No heavy interface.  
No complicated setup.  
No unnecessary features.

Just a simple way to make your clipboard more reliable.

## Installation

1. Download the latest release
2. Open the `.dmg`
3. Drag ClipFeed into your Applications folder

On first launch, macOS may block the app.

If this happens:

System Settings → Privacy & Security → **Open Anyway**

# 日本語説明

ClipFeedは、コピーした内容をうっかり上書きしてしまった時にすぐ復元できる、メニューバー常駐型のクリップボード履歴アプリです。

コピーしたテキストや画像は自動的に履歴として保存され、後から簡単に取り出すことができます。

特に以下のような場面で役立ちます。

・コピー順を間違えて前の内容を失ってしまった  
・スクリーンショットからOCRで文字をコピーした  
・作業中の一時的なテキストを保持しておきたい  

データはすべてローカルに保存され、開発者や第三者がクリップボードの内容にアクセスすることはありません。

ネットワーク通信はアプリのアップデート確認のみに使用されます。

ClipFeedは英語 / 日本語の両方に対応しています。
